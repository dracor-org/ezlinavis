The Guardian: The 100 Best Novels of All Time (16 May 2026)
https://www.theguardian.com/books/ng-interactive/2026/may/12/the-100-best-novels-of-all-time
The Guardian polled 172 authors, critics and academics for their top ten
novels, then scored each title by how often and how highly it was ranked
rather than by vote count alone, producing the top 100.
A few initial errors were corrected in the online version of the ranking
around 29 May 2026; this corrected version is used here.
The list below records ranks 1 to 100 together with every voter for each
novel, forming a network of voting communities.
Compiled by Frank Fischer (https://orcid.org/0000-0003-2419-6629),
16 May 2026, updated 21 June 2026.
Formalisation starts with first hashtag:
